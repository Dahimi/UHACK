About

UHACKS   is the ultimate hackathon experience, organized by UM6P Students for UM6P Students.
Let us solve OUR core challenges with UHACKS

What Is It

A weekend long event where UM6P students will gather to brainstorm, design and work on innovative projects addressing real world problems.
We believe tackling challenging problems is fun. Even better? Doing it with great people.

Our Plan

We’re on a mission to make the most powerful and inspiring student life from day one !
Come do the best work of your life with a passionate, committed, and collaborative team.
Come up with an innovative project to solve problems that you face as a student in UM6P.

Why UHACKS

We want to help in any way we can — which is why we’re calling on the creators, hackers, and innovators of UM6P to band together and help make a big positive impact on our University. Just hack away, and let us help you bring your ideas to life!

Here Is How It Works

  • Pick one of the Themes
  • Come up with an innovative Project
  • Win Prizes and Implement your Project

Pick One Of The Themes

Be The Next Change Maker

Our community is growing and flourishing, so decide where you want to make your impact. By choosing a track, you'll find resources and mentors to guide your hackathon journey.

Implement your Project and Win Prizes

  1. One prize per theme
  2. Top projects wil be implemented in UM6P
  3. 5000 MAD for each winner team
  4. Laptop per person (winner team)

Themes

Pick one of the following tracks

Education

With the whole world reinventing itself, innovative learning formats are more important than ever. Take your chance to be one of the creative minds redefining the future of education and taking it to the next level!

Student Organizations&Events

Student organizations and events are integral to any campus life, allowing you to get the most out of your college experience! They sets the stage for a lifetime of leaning in, and building connections that bridge barriers.

Mental Health and Wellbeing

As students, we often tend to forget about our mental health and social well-being. But under all the pressure and stress we're put through, taking care of our mental health is important and it is important now more than ever!
