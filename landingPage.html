Innovating for a Smarter University!

UHACK   is the ultimate virtual hackathon experience, organized by UM6P Students for UM6P Students.
Let us solve OUR core challenges with UHACK

What is it?

A weekend long virtual event where UM6P students will gather to brainstorm, design and work on innovative projects addressing real world problems. We believe tackling challenging problems is fun. Even better? Doing it with great people.

The top projects gets prizes and help to be implemented in UM6P.

The Problem Statement

Come up with an innovative project to solve problems that you face as a student in UM6P.

We’re on a mission to make the most powerful and inspiring student life from day one !
Come do the best work of your life with a passionate, committed, and collaborative team.
